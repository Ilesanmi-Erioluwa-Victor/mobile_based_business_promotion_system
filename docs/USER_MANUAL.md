# BizPromo — User Manual

## 1. Getting Started

Open the BizPromo website from a mobile phone, tablet or desktop browser. Create an account as either a customer or business owner.

## 2. Customer Registration

1. Click Register.
2. Enter name, email and password.
3. Select Customer.
4. Submit the form.

## 3. Business Owner Registration

1. Click Register.
2. Enter name, email and password.
3. Select Business Owner.
4. Submit the form.
5. Create a business profile.

## 4. Creating a Business Profile

1. Go to Dashboard or Create Business.
2. Enter business name, category, state, address, phone and description.
3. Upload a logo if available.
4. Save the profile.
5. Complete listing payment before the business becomes visible to customers.

## 5. Products, Promotions and Inquiries

Owners can add products from Dashboard -> Add Product. Owners can create promotions from Dashboard -> Promotions. Customers can send inquiries from the business profile page, and owners can read them from Dashboard -> View Inquiries.

## 6. Payments

### 6.1 Paying the Business Listing Fee

1. After creating your business, go to your Dashboard.
2. You will see a yellow banner: "Your business is not yet listed".
3. Click "Pay & Publish (₦2,000)".
4. A secure Paystack payment popup will appear.
5. Enter your card details or use bank transfer.
6. On success, your business will immediately appear in search results.

### 6.2 Boosting a Promotion

1. Go to Dashboard -> Promotions.
2. Find a promotion without the `★ Featured` badge.
3. Click "Boost (₦500)".
4. Complete payment on the Paystack popup.
5. Your promotion will move to the top of the homepage banner with a `★ Featured` badge.

### 6.3 Viewing Payment History

1. Go to Dashboard.
2. Scroll to Payment History.
3. The table shows reference, type (listing/boost), amount, status and date.
4. Status will show "success" for completed payments.

## 7. Searching for Businesses

Use the homepage search box or the Search page. You can filter by category and state. Only businesses that have completed listing payment are shown.

## 8. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Payment popup not opening | Check internet connection and disable popup blocker |
| Payment deducted but business not listed | Contact support with your payment reference |
| Paystack test mode | Use test card `4084084084084081`, CVV `408`, expiry `01/99`, OTP `123456` |
| Cannot login | Confirm email and password, then try again |
| Business not visible | Confirm listing payment status is successful |
